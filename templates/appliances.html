Electric Appliances

Appliance's Ownership Projection (2015-2050)

Introduction

The estimation of the relationship between penetration rate and income for each appliance based on provincial historical information was done using econometrics models and regression analysis. Then the future penetration was projected by assuming various scenarios of income growth.

Results
  • Differences between urban and rural regions will narrow
  • Current disparities between regions will tend to disappear
  • Appliance penetration growth will be led by adoption of ACs and PCs
